Cíclico
by Santi & Tuğçe
Description
Santi & Tuğçe's first full-length album, Cíclico blends elements of Afro-Cuban and Brazilian rhythms, Jazz harmonies, and Electronica sound design with warm vocals and subtle melodic lines.
