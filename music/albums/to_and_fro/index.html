To & Fro
by Santi
To & Fro
by Santi
Description
A bold exploration of sound design and musical form. As in a concerto, To & Fro introduces, develops and recapitulates multiple themes in ever-changing sonic layers. With To and Fro, Santi once again displays his omnivorous appetite for various musical influences and techniques: bottom up sound design and sequencing, polyrhythmic percussion, extended harmonies and electronica.
